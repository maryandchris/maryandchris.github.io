June 10th, 2023 is one of several "Alumni Weekends" at UVA, so we encourage our guests to begin considering travel plans early.

Getting there

  1. Charlottesville Airport: Approximately 20 minutes from downtown Charlottesville. It's possible to Uber/taxi from the airport to downtown.
  2. Richmond Airport: Approximately 1 hour from Charlottesville. You would definitely need to rent a car to get to Charlottesville.
  3. Amtrak: The Charlottesville Amtrak station (CVS) is a short walk from downtown Charlottesville. The Northeast Regional runs to CVS every day.
